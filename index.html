What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic device that processes data and performs tasks according to instructions. It is used in various areas of life, including education, business, and entertainment.

Student Learning Using Computer

The Main four Basic Parts of a Computer

Monitor

The monitor displays information from the computer.

System Unit

The system-Unit is the computer main
component,executing instruction, controlling data flow and connecting peripherals.

Keyboard

The keyboard is used to type information into the computer.

Mouse

The mouse is used to point and click on objects on the screen.

Uses of Computers in Everyday Life

  • Education: Computers help students to learn and access information.
  • Entertainment: Computers are used to watch movies, play games, and listen to music.
  • Communication: Computers allow people to communicate with others through email and social media.
